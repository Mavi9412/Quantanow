Common Questions

Your questions, answered

Is 60 days realistic for a complex product?
Yes — because we scope aggressively upfront. We define a launch-ready MVP that delivers real value, not a feature-bloated v1. Additional features are queued for the next sprint cycle.
What do I need to provide to get started?
Your vision, access to any existing assets, and one hour for the kickoff call. We take it from there — we'll interview stakeholders, audit competitors, and document everything ourselves.
How involved do I need to be day-to-day?
Minimal. You review weekly demos (30–45 min), give feedback within 48 hours, and we handle the rest. You'll never be blocked waiting for us, and we'll never be blocked waiting for you.
What happens after launch?
You get a 30-day post-launch support window, full handover documentation, and an optional growth retainer for ongoing feature development and scaling.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Absolutely. Many of our clients are founders with an idea and no technical co-founder. We act as your full technical team from day one through launch.
What tech stack do you build on?
We recommend modern, scalable stacks — typically React or Next.js frontends, Node.js or Python backends, and cloud infrastructure on AWS or GCP. We'll recommend the right fit for your use case.
