# QuantaNow Brand Assets

## Logo Files

### Primary Logo
- `quantanow-logo.svg` - Main logo for light backgrounds
- `quantanow-logo-white.svg` - Logo for dark backgrounds
- `quantanow-favicon.svg` - Favicon/app icon

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary Text**: #0A0A0A (deep black)
- **Accent Text**: #00E887 (mint green) 
- **Symbol**: Gradient from #2563EB to #00E887

### Sizes
- **Navigation**: 32px height
- **Footer**: 36px height
- **Favicon**: 32x32px

### Clear Space
Maintain at least 16px clear space around the logo on all sides.

### Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't use on backgrounds that compromise readability
- Don't recreate the logo with different fonts
- Don't change the color relationships

## Symbol Design
The QuantaNow symbol represents:
- **Circular Motion**: Continuous innovation and AI processing
- **Geometric Center**: Precision and technical excellence
- **Orbiting Elements**: Dynamic workflow and automation
- **Gradient**: Technology meets creativity
